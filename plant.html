Planting Your Blueberry Bushes

Soil Details

  • Blueberries require a low soil pH (4.5-5.5) for bountiful production

  • Install a soil pH meter to consistently monitor the acidity of your soil

  • Although it is best to begin with already acidic soil by incorporating amendments like peat moss into your soil, you may need to use additional products to adjust the pH during plant growth

Acidifying Soil

  • Hydroponic phosphoric acid solution rapidly acidifies soil and buffers pH, but requires frequent application

  • Elemental sulfur pellets lower soil pH, but require several weeks of treatment to become effective

  • Fortified sulfur solution rapidly acidifies soil and provides additional nutrients, but should be used sparingly
