Maintaining Your Blueberry Bushes

Additives

  • Like all plants, blueberry bushes require nutrients to grow

  • Although not necessarily required in properly fertilized soil, additives may be helpful in correcting nutrient imbalances and promoting accelerated growth

  • Additives should be used sparingly to avoid toxicity

Soil Additives

  • Growth-cycle optimized plant food provides necessary micronutrients (nitrogen, phosphorous, potassium) and micronutrients (trace elements) needed during each growth phase

  • Blueberries often suffer from iron deficiency, and may benefit from targeted supplementation with chelated iron

  • Applying nutrients via foliar feeding (sprayed directly onto leaves) can quickly correct general nutrient deficiencies
